My Recent Obsession with EPIC:The Musical!

Ever since the final album, ‘EPIC: The Ithaca Saga’ came out on the 25th of December, 2024, I’ve been listening to the full-length series nonstop. To give some context, ‘EPIC: The Musical’ is a breathtaking musical adaptation of Homer‚Äôs ‘The Odyssey’, transforming the legendary tale into a thrilling and emotionally charged journey through song. Created by Jorge Rivera-Herrans, the musical brings Odysseus‚Äô adventure to life with powerful storytelling, dynamic compositions, and modern theatrical elements that resonate with contemporary audiences. There are 9 short albums that tell the tale of Odysseus’s 10-year-long journey home, only being motivated to get home to his wife, Penelope. My most favourite song from the ‘Ithaca Saga’ is ‘Would You Fall in Love with Me Again’. The song carries deep emotion from both Odysseus and Penelope. The details put into the song by Jorge “Jay” Rivera-Herrans make the song more meaningful and deeper. For example, my most favourite detail is in the bridge of the song, Penelope (voiced by Anna Lea) sings the word “waiting” seven times for 20 seconds. This is to signify the fact that she waited for Odysseus’s return home for 7 sagas(albums) and 20 years.

Overall, Jay the mastermind behind this whole musical is such a genius for incorporating small details within every song and album from the previous releases. It makes listening to the series such an enjoyable and exciting experience. I also love keeping up with Jay and the other casts content on their social medias of behind the scenes and more.
